Prawn Linguine

Everyone needs a great, go-to pasta dish that the ycan turn to in a pinch. For me, that dish is a Chipotle Prawn Linguine. This dish is a fantasitc, fresh and psicy pasta dish that can come together in half an hour, and today I'm going to teach you how to do just that!

Ingredients

  • Prawns
  • Linguine
  • Fresh Tomatoes
  • Chipotle Paste
  • Tomato Paste
  • Lemon
  • Onions
  • Olive Oil
  • Chilli
  • Garlic
  • White wine

Steps

  1. Marinate the prawns in the chipotle paste.
  2. Heat some oil in a pot on a medium heat. Saute the onions or shallots in a pot until soft and then add the chilli and garlic and let them cook for a further 3 minutes.
  3. Add the tomato paste to the pot and cover the onion, chilli and garlic. After this add the fresh tomatoes to the pot and allow them to break down into a sauce. Allow this sauce to simmer for a few minutes. Add white wine and cook until reduced.
  4. In a separate pan, bring water to a boil and add the linguine. Follow the package instructions to cook the pasta until it is al dente.
  5. Once the pasta is al dente, add to the sauce and mix. Do this for a few minutes until pasta is fully cooked. After 2 minutes serve and enjoy.
