Tacos

Tacos! Tacos are one of the most popular foods across the world. Originating from Mexico, the taco is an incredibly versitile food, and there is no one definitve way to make a taco as you can make them with any filling of your choice. Today I'm going to teach yo a recipe for vegan tacos that anyone can enjoy.

Ingredients

  • Corn Tortillas
  • Pinto Beans/Refried Beans
  • Mushrooms
  • Cheese
  • Salsa
  • Lime
  • Coriander
  • Onions/Shallots
  • Chilli
  • Oil
  • Garlic
  • Chipotle Paste
  • Cumin
  • Cayenne Pepper
  • Paprika
  • Garlic Powder
  • Onion Powder

Steps

  1. Heat some oil in a pan on a medium heat. Saute the onions or shallots in a pan until soft and then add the chilli and garlic and let them cook for a further 3 minutes.
  2. Add the pinto beans to the pan and mash them. After a paste has form add your psices and chipotle paste allow the paste to cook for a few minutes on a medium heat. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  3. In a separate pan, saute the mushrooms on a medium heat. Add the seasonings to the mushroom and salt and pepper to taste. Once the mushrooms have browned take the pan off the heat.
  4. Heat up the tortilla in the pan on a low to medium heat for 30 seconds and then add some of the refried beans and mushrooms. Then add some salsa, cheese, lime and coriander, and enjoy.
